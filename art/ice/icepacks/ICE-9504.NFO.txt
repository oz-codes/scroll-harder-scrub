15,014. With the  new release of  iCEView 0.64,  it now supports some new  features,
15,01   such as  multiple file-tagging  and viewing.  But most  importantly, iCEView
15,01   now supports  external viewers.  This can be set up in the ICEVIEW.CNF file,
15,01   and allows you to do  such things as add in ability to  play *.S3M and *.MOD
15,01   with DMP,  *.JPG with Qpeg (the  recommended JPeg viewer*), etc.  This opens
15,01   the  possibility of  setting it up  to even run  Word Perfect on *.WPD, etc.
15,01   iCEView does not perform a CRC Check on the external viewers.

15,01   * Qpeg is available at ftp.wustl.edu   /pub/msdos_uploads/graphics
