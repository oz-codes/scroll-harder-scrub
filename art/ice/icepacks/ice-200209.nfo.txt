15,01ACiD and Teklordz luminary Smooth.  He's crawled out of his mudhut in Hawaii
15,01after departing the ansi scene back 1994.  With his return also comes the
15,01reincarnation of his BBS, the newly dubbed Dungeon 3
15,01(telnet://dungeon3.darktech.org).  Beware of raging Smoothasaurus and
15,01Enzolic.

15,01He's a fantasy artist who enjoys painting fairies, like Peter Pan!

15,01magic shovel (the legendary Garden Tool of Doom, +5, +7 vs plants, +9 vs
15,01small animals, special: Fireball (3/day)) he managed to defend himself
15,01from the cunning reptile.  It was a vicious battle to the death, but
15,01Darkmage was victorious.  He is currently recuperating in a dark cave with
15,01a PBR (http://www.pabst.com) and a hottie with a sword.
