15,011. Just like Heinz Ketchup (http://root88.ice.org/root.jpg), we're a little
15,01   slow out of the bottle this month.  We would like to blame Neitzsche for
15,01   the delay.  And since we have no new members this month, we might as
15,01   well toss some more love towards J-Tec.

15,012. We did a massive server migration this month across the US to our new
15,01   server, mango.ice.org, as hinted in last month's interview.  After
15,01   Slothy's move out to California from Virginia, the iCE server was
15,01   feeling blue and missed him.  So Farmy put together a new spankin
15,01   server out in California on an even fatter pipe.  Hopefully the site
15,01   feels faster now.  But we must point out that Farmy is possibly the
15,01   greatest person in the world.  Even though ever since he was audited by
15,01   the IRS when he was 15, he does get a little TOO excited when people
15,01   ask for shell accounts.

15,013. Much like Heinz Ketchup, Darkmage has even more snakes in his yard.  And
15,01   Greater Evil is working on contracting the Black Death in time for
15,01   Christmas.  Hey, speaking of Christmas, guess what the most popular
15,01   product on our Christmas lists is?  That's right, Krispy Kreme donuts!
15,01   Unfortunately they don't ward off speeding tickets...

15,014. We're all gunning for Stereophoenix, who, like Heinz Ketchup, comes in
15,01   a new squeezable container as part of a fresh marketing perspective.
15,01   Thankfully, though, Stereophoenix isn't being sold in a frightening
15,01   green color!  And he doesn't have much in common with the random guy who
15,01   comes from 90 degrees away to grab Jason and piggyback him through the
15,01   atmosphere in Jason X.

15,015. iCE has some big and exciting new plans for our website in the coming
15,01   months.  We would love to get one or two guys or gals to help with
15,01   bringing it all together.  So if you are a web coding guru who would
15,01   love to be a part of our little family (and know how to find your way
15,01   around a MySQL database), drop us a line at apply@ice.org with a bit
15,01   about yourself and some sites you've done.

15,016. Halloween is really creepy and scary, which makes totally unlike Heinz
15,01   Ketchup.  This is different from "hell on earth" though, which
15,01   apparently describes TonchyZ's school as of late.  I bet they don't
15,01   serve Heinz Ketchup there...

15,017. Did you know that in Swedish, the term used for bullshitting is
15,01   literally translated as "word shitting" ? If you're a member of the
15,01   awesome DeviantArt, our apologies if this is old news.
15,01   (http://web.archive.org/web/20030613144558/http://www.ice.org/~slothy/oldnews
15,01.jpg)
