15,01The Wind

15,01Blow, o wind, blow, and pierce my soul:
15,01You rend the fibers of my heart.
15,01Blow, o wind, blow, and pierce my soul:
15,01You pull my soiled mind apart.

15,01Blow, o wind, blow, and clense my soul:
15,01Set flight my sins on Zephyr's breath.
15,01Blow, o wind, blow, and clense my soul:
15,01Oh save me from eternal death.

15,01Blow, o wind, blow, and heal my soul:
15,01My newly stainless heart you mend.
15,01Blow, o wind, blow, and heal my soul:
15,01My crimes and pains away you send.
