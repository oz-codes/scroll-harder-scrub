15,011. Well, we made it!  It's our 10th anniversary of packs!  Although iCE was
15,01   started in 1990, we began releasing a monthy archive of our artwork in
15,01   08/92.  That makes this pack our 121st iCE Pack - the start of our 11th
15,01   year of packs!  Over the years we've released 4,576 pieces of art from
15,01   693 members. That comes to over 400 megabytes of artwork!

15,01   Over the years, the group has seen some great times and some low times,
15,01   but we've never missed a single pack.  We've met some really cool people
15,01   over the years, some of whom have been trickling back to say hello once
15,01   again.  We've had numerous parties, countless fun times, and we plan to
15,01   do it all over again for another decade of packs!  We hope you'll be
15,01   there with us for the next 10 years!  We couldn't do it without you guys!

15,012. Who shot the J.R.?  Who stole the string of pearls?  Which key opens the
15,01   the secret door?  These things and more can be yours for only $19.95.
15,01   Order now and receive a free Radioactive Man #1 comic book ($100 Value)!

15,013. We would love to thank DeviantArt for the Best Art Group award from their
15,01   2002 Devvies Awards!  We couldn't be more proud of the honor, and we
15,01   thank everyone at DeviantArt for the award!  Let us take this time to say
15,01   that Breed and Raster are both really amazing art groups and you should
15,01   go check out their packs if you haven't yet. 

15,013a. We'd also be negligent if we didn't give spx a huge pat on the back for
15,01    his awards from the Deviant Art Devvies!  Congrats on winning Most
15,01    Original IndyArt and Best Overall Wallpaper!  Way to go spx!  Congrats
15,01    to all the other winners on DeviantArt as well, just don't mind us if
15,01    we shamelessly plug our crew :) 

15,014. You may have noticed that Trip went balls-to-the-wall this month and
15,01   organized a full-scale ansi assault!  We'd love to welcome back the
15,01   hallowed Marshal Law and the omnicient Tempus Thales!  It's great to
15,01   have you guys back for our 10th Anniversary!  Way to show the newbies
15,01   what the old school rusty fellas can create!
