15,011. We have some new artists to welcome to iCE yet again!  This month we're
15,01   proud to include Knives & Green Water to the group.  KGW joins us after
15,01   winning our contest where he sold the most magazine subscriptions door
15,01   to door.  He has a really fresh art style and we look forward to all of
15,01   his future contributions to the group! 

15,01   Also this month is Primal_r, who joins the group after a long and
15,01   fruitful time participating on tiles.ice.org.  He's been really patient
15,01   and dedicated to getting in and it's finally paid off.  We're very lucky
15,01   to have him, and we have high hopes that he'll manage to eat the most
15,01   marshmallows at next year's New Year's bash! 

15,012. Check it out!  As promised, this month we have an interview with
15,01   everyone's favorite master engraver, DaVinci.  Being one of our 
15,01   "old farts", he has a bit of wisdom to share, even if he thinks our
15,01   music just sounds like a bunch of hiss and popping!

15,014. Owing it all to a terrible fear of Jombogan, Helm accidentally realized
15,01   that he was in fact a member of the group and promptly started
15,01   submitting art.  Hence you see the cool pixel art in this pack.  If you
15,01   have any complaints, talk to Gecko. 

15,015. We have something special this month!  MrMister is releasing a Game Boy
15,01   Advance intro he coded, along with the full source code.  If you're not
15,01   lucky enough to have the equipment to load roms onto your GBA, you can
15,01   download one of the Game Boy Advance emulators
15,01   (http://vboy.emuhq.com/downloads.shtml) and check out his intro.
15,01   Nice work, MrMister, and it's great that you've giving out the code.

15,016. Farmy, in an astounding move, continued to keep things running smoothly
15,01   on our servers.  That guy is really cool.
