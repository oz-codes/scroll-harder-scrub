15,01                The UprisingNet General Rules and Guidelines
15,01        
15,01        The rules for The UprisingNet are Short and Simple.  

15,01  1) The UprisingNet is designed to be an Art Net.  It is 100% Dedicated to
15,01     Art and to the R.C./+. Art Group.

15,01  2) There is to be NO bashing of any sort of any Art Group or any People.
15,01    
15,01  3) Only R.C.A. Members will have access to the Member Only Conferences.

15,01  4) No excessive Profanity will be Tolerated.

15,01  5) The Moderator has the right to Remove or Declare ANY conversation or
15,01     Subject off topic.

15,01  6) I reserve the right to change these rules without notice at any time.
15,01   
15,01  7) Anyone who cannot or does not comply with the Rules of the Network Will
15,01     be Blacklisted from the Net and possibly the BBS's that carry it.
