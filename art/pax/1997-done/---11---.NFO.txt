15,01                                  ON THE EDGE





15,01 Still standing on the edge, we do.These days it can be felt especially
15,01 strong, yeah, you can loose your poise and fall anytime. How many of them
15,01 there are? How many possible turns, deadends, cliffs and weak bridges?
15,01 The edge between years, lasting for centuries, for millenia.. The edge
15,01 between alcohol and new job, between love and suicide. For a well-balanced
15,01 man that's his anxiety and emotional splashes, for one with a devolish flame
15,01 burning inside his eyes - a new turbulent stream giving fresh blood and
15,01 wings.  And we are dancing in the flame, we, who always live on the edge,
15,01 borned between two evils, between seven winds.
