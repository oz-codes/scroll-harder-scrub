14,01  00Introduction
14,01----------------
14,01    00After many months of planning, programming, testing, and adding new
00,01features, our eagerly awaited viewer is ready for release to the public.
00,01We intend MULTI-VIEW to be a facility in which all of our monthly pack
00,01releases can simply and efficiently be viewed.

00,01    15MULTI-VIEW was originally intended as an enhanced version of K-View.
15,01Later, we realized that it had far surpassed it's predecessor, warranting
15,01a new name.  XV was written in 100% C from scratch, excluding the SVGA
15,01routines (Jordan Hargraphix) and the GIF routines (Kyle Powell). Some of the
15,01ANSI conversion code, in addition, was based on Watchman's KV code.

15,01    00Although we have found our program to be bug free among us who have
00,01been playing with this code, since, let's see.. December.. we are sure that
00,01you will come across some things that we have missed.. We also regret having
00,01to leave out some other functions such as full mouse support and animation
00,01mode due to singificant bugs as of this time.. expect these functions to be
00,01available in the next update of the viewer as well as full support for .RIP
00,01and .EXE files
