15,01                             Lost Innocence
15,01                            ----------------

15,01Memories grow faint in time,
15,01Thy soul, alone, grows cold and dies,
15,01Tangled secrets deep inside,
15,01Thy face a masque to hide the lies.

15,01Scars from lashings long ago,
15,01Covered o'er yet still they show,
15,01Truth a ghost you dare not know,
15,01Thy heart stopped beating yet blood still flows.

15,01Youthful dreams have all but flown,
15,01Stolen while thy eyes still shone,
15,01Tedious existence trudges on,
15,01Innocence lost, memories gone.
