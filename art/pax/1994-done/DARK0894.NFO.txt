15,01-----[ Filenames ]-----------------------------------------------------

15,01DARK art files will now be named in the following manner:

15,01                              XXXXX-##.DRK

15,01XXXXX  represents five characters  for artist name.  ## is a hexadecimal
15,01number representing the number of releases that artist has made to date.
15,01With the  increase of conflicts involving  initials, it's become obvious
15,01that the old Mirage file  naming scheme is  out-of-date.  Hopefully this
15,01new method  will help  to alleviate  that problem.  We're hoping that if
15,01any other art groups find this  convention logical, they feel free to go
15,01ahead and use it.
