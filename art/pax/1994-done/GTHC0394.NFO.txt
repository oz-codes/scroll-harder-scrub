15,01    o My first NFO file for Gothic.  Enjoy ... - Aphex Twin

15,01    o First off, the bad news: Forlorn Extender has left the group to
15,01      pursue, uh ... well ... we don't know.  Word has it he's possibly
15,01      leaving the scene but that is as yet unconfirmed.

15,01    o I have replaced STiLE as one of the two Canadian Directors, and Bane
15,01      is the new US Director, replacing The Calibre who went to ACiD.

15,01    o A new position has been created: "Administrative Officer".  STiLE
15,01      and Sinned Soul, who have helped out the group in many ways over the
15,01      past month, are now holding this title.

15,01   .o We're sad to announce the break-up of TRiBE.  After Shihear was
15,01      caught ripping off a Quasar ANSI he left the scene to compose "a very
15,01      long message" explaining how he was framed; TRiBE quickly fell apart
15,01      due to a lack of organization.  Coming to Gothic from TRiBE is Jocko.
15,01      Welcome to the group.

15,01    o We have two promising new artists joining us this month in Fizban
15,01      and Ground Zero; we hope to see some good stuff out of you two...
15,01      welcome to Gothic.  Blade has been promoted from apprenticeship to a
15,01      full VGA artist, and Xerf, a newcomer to the scene, is our new VGA
15,01      apprentice.  Critical Perspective has also joined us as a VGA
15,01      artist.

15,01    o Dual Standard has joined us as a courier, and is handling the boards
15,01      out in western Canada.  His new US counterparts are Cybermage, Cynic
15,01      Cipher, and Omega Redd.

15,01    o We have a new viewer this month -- Bloodview, coded by SiDS, who is
15,01      the latest member of our coding division.  Both GV and BV will be
15,01      included in future packs; use whichever you prefer.

15,01      A new version GothicView is being released in the pack; all RIP
15,01      functions are now working correctly.  BIG thanks to Skull Leader for
15,01      the last minute help.  It's really appreciated.

15,01    o GothicDraw will be released sometime next month.  It's a new 800
15,01      line ANSI editor with built-in VGA, being programmed by Sinned Soul.
15,01      I've tried out an early beta, and it's already starting to look
15,01      pretty cool.

15,01    o Forgemaster has become a Gothic musician, joining us from ACiD.
15,01      Timelord and Basehead are currently working on a musicdisk that will
15,01      be released within the coming months.
