15,01                          00DARK NEWSLETTER : APRIL 1997

11,01Intro15 11---

15,01       Expectations can be a real pain in the ass.  The art scene is, to
15,01       many of us, a hobby.  Yet the groups which release their packs to
15,01       the underground are constantly expected to surpass their previous
15,01       efforts, and are reviled or considered "dead or dying" if they
15,01       don't.

15,01       This is particularly true of the older, longer-lived art groups
15,01       which, if they're like DARK, stay together more out of respect
15,01       and companionship for one another than pleasing the #ansi 
15,01       regulars.

15,01       Don't take this as an excuse to slack off; that's not the case.
15,01       But we'd like to be able to relax and have fun drawing, good or
15,01       bad, and not have to put up with the potshots and unfriendly 
15,01       fire from the sidelines, where the envious or uninspired seek
15,01       to exact what little satisfaction they can by slandering us.
