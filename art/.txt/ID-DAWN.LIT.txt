15,01                          Rising of the Dawn 
15,01                          ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~ 



15,01From a time of darkness a child was born.
15,01With skill and might was he forlorned.

15,01Through many of ages past's his youth
15,01and in time learned lessons of valor and truth.
15,01Merely defeating his foes and conquering all his fears,
15,01was but a petty task through his teenage years.
15,01Now in a land of corruption and defiled souls, his
15,01fiery spirit yearned for control.
15,01Like a lantern in the night was this 
15,01one's will burning bright.

15,01Now all eye's turn to marvel in wonder for this one's
15,01glory and splendor. And as the time for his deliverance
15,01nears all who opposed him begin to tremble in fear. 
15,01For they all know well his unforgiving nature, and 
15,01ruthless the path he travels in hatred.

15,01But peace of heart he holds near, for he knows calm 
15,01dignity is a hidden goal that many in past days have 
15,01toiled to sow.

15,01So be forewarned. For from the depths of darkness
15,01to the damned's despite emerges the dawn in fiery might!
