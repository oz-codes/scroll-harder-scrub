15,01                                   Cathedral
15,01                                   ~~~~~~~~~

15,01                              Believe in angels...


15,01                         Darkened spires loom overhead,
15,01                         Raindrops pelting tired stone,
15,01                    The tears of myriad slaughtered saints,
15,01                       Seraphim forged of flesh and bone.

15,01                     For centuries standing cold and grey,
15,01                        Cloaked in shadows deep withal,
15,01                          Wind-whipped and rain-swept,
15,01               Haunted voices echo through haunted, empty halls.

15,01                       Icon, testament, devoid and cold,
15,01                       Not spared by deity or time alike,
15,01                     Unfeeling Heaven stares loftily down,
15,01                        Shadows rule and swallow light.

15,01                     Deep in the courtyard bleak and grim,
15,01                            Carved in ancient stone,
15,01                    Are etched these words in letters bold,
15,01                            By ancient hand unknown:

15,01                    "Incline thine ear and hear these words,
15,01                     Neither fire, rust nor heathen swords,
15,01                      Shall lay to waste Cathedral's wall,
15,01                     Nor despoiler cause her ramparts fall.

15,01                   For faith here dies and hope doth perish,
15,01                 When stone and foundation divinely cherished,
15,01                          Decay in rubble all around,
15,01                     And Cathedral crumbles to the ground."
