15,011.  Greetings  and welcome  to the  results of the  month-long  iCE spectacular
15,01celebration of the iCEPACK's fifth anniversary.  August's pack brought everyone
15,01out in  droves to design eye-candy  fitting of a world-class  art organization.
15,01Everyone  thought  last month  was an  overachieving month,  but this pack  has
15,01broken all the records!  Keep reading for more information.

15,012.  As you probably have noticed by now, this pack is an EXTRA SPECIAL iCEPACK!
15,01This pack,  August 1997,  marks the  60th edition of the iCEPACK.   The iCEPACK
15,01has been released consistantly  every month around  the first of the  month for
15,0160 months straight,  dating all the way  back to five years ago with the August
15,011992 pack.   iCE has existed  for longer  than five  years,  of course,  but we
15,01did not  start releasing  packs until 08/92,  following the lead of  the  first
15,01group to release packs, MiRAGE, with the MiRAGE compendium (MiRAGE later merged
15,01into iCE, in December 1992).

15,012a. This pack is  a record breaking pack in a  couple of respects.   First off,
15,01and most  importantly,  iCE is the  first group  to celebrate the  release of a
15,0160th pack.   While MiRAGE,  and then  ACiD,  released  packs before us,  MiRAGE
15,01merged into  iCE in 12/92 and  ACiD has missed  several packs  over  the years.
15,01Secondly, there never has been a pack of this sheer volume.   We have well over
15,01six  megabytes of artwork, with an almost unheard of range of styles (ANSI/VGA,
15,01plus animations too!).

15,013.  This pack features  a couple  of special sections that  are part of our 5th
15,01Anniversary Pack celebration.   First,  the group has rallied to bridge old and
15,01new by taking a  few selected ANSIs from the August  1992 iCEPACK and re-mixing
15,01them.   Re-mixing  creates a joint-release  that spans  time itself.   Look for
15,01some of your favorite iCE ANSIs from the past re-mixed by some of your favorite
15,01iCE artists of today!

15,013b. Secondly, we have invited  several of our inactive iCE members to take part
15,01in the celebrations by dusting off their  copies of TheDraw or VGA programs and
15,01create a brand new release for this pack!  The "oldskool" section includes work
15,01this month from Liquid Image,  P-Chan,  Tempus Thales,  Sober,  and Iodine (aka
15,01Posyden).  But we plan to feature more "oldskool" artwork in the remaining 1997
15,01packs as we continue to  celebrate the 1992-7 5th Anniversary.  Expect works in
15,01the future from the  above artists  and others like..  well..  it'll just  have
15,01to be a surprise!  Enjoy the works.

15,014.  The propoganda department conned several wayward artists into thinking that
15,01iCE is a free-love nudist colony and signed them up this month.   The ANSI team
15,01welcomes back  The Green Hornet to the group,  and the VGA department opens its
15,01doors to Night Daemon.  The Members department yanked in Roland before he could
15,01escape, as well.  Welcome to all!
