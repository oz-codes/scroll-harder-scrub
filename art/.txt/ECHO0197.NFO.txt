15,01    Welcome to another exciting and eventful month here, at Dark Illustrated!
15,01It's me again, The 4Th Disciple taking over for the ever busy Xypher Matryx
15,01but don't worry good ol' Xyph will be back next month.  This year marks the
15,013rd year of releases from Dark Illustrated, and the 4th year of group operation.
15,01We've come a long way, haven't we? :)

11,01group news

11,01    15This month brought back desalvo from the ranks of retirement to draw his 
15,01heart out once more with Dark!  Great to see you back man.  More great news
15,01allowed Carnifex to fix that pesky "I don't have time to draw that much" 
15,01attitude.  The 5 huge ansis from him this month show that he may be getting  
15,01older, stinkier, and uglier but he's not quitting. j/k :)
15,01    From this months release, we can see that Xypher Matryx is indeed still in
15,01recovery from the ansi beating that 4thds gave to him a few months back.  Don't 
15,01worry man, we believe in you.. get well soon. 
15,01    Another surprise this month was the tag team of Burnout and Prison Breaker
15,01who released Dark's all ascii first pack.  Who knew that ascii could look so
15,01good coming from 2 VGA artists... 

11,01group meet

15,01    At this months Dark meet, there were many surprises.  Armageddon Donut
15,01showed up in his patented suit, however much to the chagrine of members he had
15,01forgot his pants.  Burnout took this opportunity to have some fun with good
15,01ol' "Chicken legs".  Donut reacted with a swift peck to Bo's chin.
15,01    The Green Hornet, obsessed in taking back his "GQ" image from Donut wore
15,01an Arthurian garb with a wig and everything.  When asked about this, his 
15,01response was " I'm gonna dunk that donut ".  Equinox, was found to secretly be
15,01the lead singer of Raggapuke.  An underground Reggae band who does bad Vanilla
15,01Ice makeovers with a hint of reggae.  Eq, was quickly beaten and left for dead. 
15,01    Mimes, Dark's official cheerleader showed up with an army of small squishy
15,01hamster fetus's ready to battle even the strongest of stenched.  These little
15,01creatures were left to fend for themselves when an overgrown Reddeath 
15,01intimidated them by his secret weapon, the "silent treatment".. it was later
15,01found that he was not infact mute, as once thought by all.  "He talks!"
15,01    Xypher found a soul mate in one of the hamster fetus's, and soon we should
15,01be seeing little hairy Xypher juniors runnin' around.  Damn, that's ooglay.
