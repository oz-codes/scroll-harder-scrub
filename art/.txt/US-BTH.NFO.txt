15,01     14of people, but its the only way to break out of the pathetic world15 14of
15,01     14standard 320x200x256 graphics without selling out to the DirectX Windows95
15,01     14trend and without writing support for every video card individualy.  ( I
15,01     14will not use someone elses graphics library ).  The latest version of
15,01     14SciTech Display Doctor supports just about every video card on the planet,
15,01     14so if you install that you should be fine.  VESA 2.0 offers such a drama-  
15,01     14tic speed improvement for normal Windows operation and DOS games I don't   
15,01     14know why anyone wouldn't want to install it.  Anyway, these are the cards  
14,01     that I tested it on.15  14If your card doesn't directly support VESA 2.0 then  
14,01  15   14make sure the SDD control panel has a Linear Frame Buffer setup and
15,01     14working.
