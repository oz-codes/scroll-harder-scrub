15,01Introduction
15,01        
15,01        Havok merged with a group by the name of iLLUSiON this month, 
15,01        they show some pretty good potential and good organization,
15,01        and should make a nice addition to the group.  We've also 
15,01        introduced a new ansi editing program written by Techie, but
15,01        upon the author's request, only an unregistered alpha version 
15,01        has been included with the pack.  I'd also like to aknowlege
15,01        that Mr. Corruption, one of the newly gained senior members
15,01        from the iLLUSiON merge, will be adding to the Newsletter 
15,01        this month.
15,01        
15,01New Members:
15,01        New members include everyone from iLLUSiON, too numerous to 
15,01        list.  Just compare the new member list to the old. :)
15,01        
15,01Members resigning:

15,01        One resignation has occured this month, Perkman left to join
15,01        Vi, but apparently was not accepted, so his current affiliations
15,01        are unknown.

15,01News

15,01        As I said before, Havok is introducing a new ANSi editor,
15,01        once that supports 3800 lines of editing, surpassing 
15,01        Thedraw (tm) 100 line limit.  To top it off, it is written
15,01        in pure Assembler, so the editor is a whopping 7k with
15,01        the documentation.
15,01        An Application form generator has been included this month,
15,01        if you have talent, feel free to apply.

15,01Artwork: 

15,01        In RiP art this month, we have gained a new rip artist from
15,01        iLLUSiON, Asrael.  So hopefully you will be seeing a lot
15,01        more rip art from Havok.

15,01        Our VGA department is getting even better with new acquisitions
15,01        of several artists from iLLUSiON.  Perkman has left Havok, his
15,01        current whereabouts is unknown.

15,01        Due to some personal problems, The Replicant has only shown
15,01        one piece of art this month.  Nightbreed showed some outstanding
15,01        work again, and is continuing to improve his artwork.  The Stalker
15,01        has rejoined Havok, and shows that his skills have improved 
15,01        imensly since he left Havok.  Sword Weaver as well had some
15,01        personal business to attend to, i.e., filling out college 
15,01        applications, so his artwork is limited this month, but still
15,01        of terrific quality.

15,01        The Lit department is again very solid, with Zaphod contributing 
15,01        another large quantities of work, as well as quality.  Destiny
15,01        has continued to show her originality, improving her style of
15,01        'colorful' poetry.

15,01Coding:

15,01        version is included with this archive.  Also, Techie has improved
