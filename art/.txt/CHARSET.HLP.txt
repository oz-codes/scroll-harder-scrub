15,01CHARSET: Display all characters of current screen font. Ver 1.5 Apr 16 1993
15,01Copyright (c) 1990-1993 by Yossi Gil, P.O.Box 3148, Jerusalem, ISRAEL.

15,01     Usage: charset [-p]

15,01Will display a 16x80 screen comprising of a table of all 256 different chars
15,01in current screen font, text demos (the quick brown fox..., figures, currency
15,01symbols, and miscellaneous other characters), business graphic, ASCII controls,
15,01arrows, boxes, mathematical symbols, dots, etc. CHARSET is useful for
15,01demonstrating and examining a screen font.

15,01FLAGS
15,01   -p          Wait for a key hit before terminating.
15,01   -h          Display this message.
15,01   -H          Display this message.
15,01   -?          Display this message.
