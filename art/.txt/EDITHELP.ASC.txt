15,01                                 EDIT HELP

15,01There are a variety of editor commands.  All of them may be used either as
15,01single letters from the edit menu or as a slash ("/") + the letter while
15,01entering text, that is, entering "/S" on a blank line will save the message.

15,01Letter  Purpose
15,01------  -------
15,01S       Save
15,01A       Abort
15,01E       Enter edit mode
15,01C       Continue
15,01F       Fix line
15,01L       List message
15,01N       New (clear)
15,01D       Delete line
15,01I       Insert line
15,01B       Backup (fix)
15,01T       Change title
15,01R       Search and replace
15,01M<text> Center text

15,01The editor supports word-wrap and backup, that is, if you press the backspace
