15,01This lesson is for guitarist that wants to learn to SHRED.
15,01If you don't, then don't read it.  I don't want to read
15,01email about how shred has no feel, if that's the way you
15,01think, then keep it to yourself, we don't want to hear it.

15,01This is going to be a series of lessons for the serious
15,01guitarist, the guitarist that want's to get the most out of
15,01his/her fingers and guitar.  These lessons will be posted
15,01a bit erradically (sp?).  So have patience, I will do as many
15,01as I can.

15,01The first thing you will want to do is to limber up your fingers
15,01before you start.  These exercises are an excellent way to
15,01limber up.  BE WARNED!!!  If you experience pain, stop, rest,
15,01stretch out your fingers then go back, DO NOT practice with
15,01pain, it's the worst thing you can do.
