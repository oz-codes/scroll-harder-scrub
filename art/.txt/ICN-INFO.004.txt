15,01        For close to a year, CCi, or Cybercrime International Network, was
15,01   the lifeblood of iCE.  It provided the communication needed to support a
15,01   group of that size.  In the early summer of 1993, Midnight Sorrow, the CCi
15,01   founder and administrator, abruptly quit any and all ties with the net as
15,01   well as the rest of the scene.  For three months, iCE waited for the net
15,01   to be reinstated while the group was floundering directly due to lack of
15,01   communication.

15,01              Enter iNSANiTY.
15,01    iNSANiTY (iCN) was originally created by Tempus Thales, and quickly died.
15,01   December 17, 1993, xerobe, with the help of both Metal Head, and Tempus
15,01   Thales reinstated iCN as the *ONLY* official network of iCE(tm)
15,01   Productions. iCN was created to solely support iCE members. At its
15,01   creation ALL bases will be restricted to iCE members only, this will
15,01   continue UNTIL iCN is fully operational at which point public bases will
15,01   be added. Any board that is run by an iCE member is guaranteed a spot in
15,01   ICN, but if for any reason you do not comply with  the simple rules, shall
15,01   be purged. iCN #2 Pack introduces a few public bases, for any users on
15,01   previous carriers of iCN to read. In addition any iCE member who does not
15,01   run a board of his/her own may submit to myself a recommendation for a
15,01   bulletin board (possible a board they are cosysop on) which they would
15,01   like to read iCN from, each member will have the right to select one
15,01   board, which will be approved by xerobe. As time passes and as the
15,01   need arises MORE bases will be added, I always welcome suggestions, post
15,01   them on ICN-SUGG, as I will take every suggestion into consideration.
15,01    iCN's main purpose is to provide each and every iCE member with the
15,01   ability to keep up-to-date with iCE news and information, for iCE members
15,01   to be able to discuss freely their views and feelings about iCE. Once
15,01   iCN adds on public subs iCN will also be a network to contact ALL iCE
15,01   members (hopefully). If you are an iCE member but do not run a board,
15,01   and WOULD like to have access to iCN contact me with what board you would
15,01   like to admit to iCN. 
15,01    iCN will designate 1 hub per country, this will provide order, and make
15,01   the network almost non-lagged.  All nodes will be expected to poll their
15,01   respective hubs seven times per week, if you will not be  able  to  do
15,01   this please contact  Metal Head or Myself. ICN will be a solely iCE
15,01   support / graphics network,  consisting of  a minimum number  of bases.
