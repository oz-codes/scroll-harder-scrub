15,01What the hell is this?

15,01SK-MENU1.ZIP is the first of two files, the 2nd being released in the next
15,01iCEPack.  Each will contain 10 ANSI files of many of the Image Comic Book
15,01Characters.  The individual files are named after thier respecitive content
15,01for easy selection and use.  They're not labled for specific Menu's or Stat
15,01files, so the choice to use whatever picture for whatever use is up totally
15,01to you.

15,01Why the hell are you doing this?

15,01I'd been asked to do Menus or Stat files for several boards and softwares,
15,01not to mention having to create a set for MY board.  While working on the MY
15,01Menus, I found out just what an undertaking this was.  Needless to say, the
15,01thought of doing about 100 of these type things for everyone didn't sound
15,01like a whole lot of fun.  I decided to make ONE set for anyone interested.

15,01What the hell do I do with this?

15,01Simple.  Take the ANSi of the character you want, load it up in The Draw,
15,01type in the commands, stats, MCI, whatever.  That's it.  You pretty much
15,01understand the process I'm sure.  (If not, fuck you, you stupid moron! ;)
15,01To help those of you less inclined in these sort of things, I've included
15,01a TDFONT.TDF file you can use for the headers if you like.
