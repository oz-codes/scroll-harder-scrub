15,01     Imagine how communications pioneer Guglielmo Marconi felt when radio
15,01     came along to render his wireless telegraph obsolete.

15,01     That's about where Patrick Clawson and his colleagues at TeleGrafix
15,01     Communications Inc. found themselves several years ago when the World
15,01     Wide Web burst onto the scene.

15,01     At the time, TeleGrafix's RIPscrip software was favored by thousands
15,01     of computer bulletin board operators across the country as a fast,
15,01     easy method for dressing up subscribers' computer screens with
15,01     artwork, logos and other adornments.

15,01     Then came the Web explosion of 1994, offering a sexy new way to
15,01     transmit not only text--but also video, sounds and point-and-click
15,01     commands--over the Internet.  Suddenly, anything that wasn't on the
15,01     Web and created with its unique software technology seemed passe.
15,01     Bulletin board operators began attaching themselves to the Web and
15,01     RIPscrip salves withered.

15,01     "It was a tidal wave," said Clawson, a broadcasting industry veteran
15,01     who had purchased a large stake in TeleGrafix in 1994 and became its
15,01     president and chief executive, just as the wave struck.

15,01     Now TeleGrafix is trying for a comeback.  Now joined with Fire
15,01     Productions, the world-wide leader in ANSI text-based graphics
15,01     creation, the Winchester, Va., firm released a product on which
15,01     its hopes are pinned, a test version of a RIPscrip package that
15,01     lets Internet users tap into university and government databases
15,01     using simple, eye-catching Web-like graphics and commands.

15,01     Many of these big computer systems have been linked to the Internet
15,01     for years.  But they have not been easily reached via the Web because
15,01     of the high cost of converting the systems to the Web format, Clawson
15,01     said.  Moreover, even if there are connections, the Web merely takes
15,01     users to the doorway of these older data storehouses.  Many of
15,01     today's new Web aficionados would be lost inside, because difficult
15,01     "telnet" computer technology commands must be typed to search for
15,01     files, copy information and carry out other tasks.
