00,01  15. 14The Alliance 15. 14A New Force in 619 15. 14Newsletter Issue #1 15. 14July 17, 1994 15.
00,01------------------------------------------------------------------------------

15,01         Welcome, to the first ever Alliance Newsletter. First off, we should
15,01tell you a little about ourselves. The Alliance is, or rather was a three grou
15,01combination, the Children of Judas, Epinicion, and Sonic. It was never seen to
15,01be a merger, but a combined effort to bring new talent into the scene, just
15,01something that 619 can say, "HEY! We've got one more group that looks like it'
15,01going to fail!" but we won't, we are dedicated and just out to have some good
15,01old fun. Now, we will compete, especially with our main rival group here in
15,01619, Pro. With all of the combined talent and connections that the three group
15,01had seperately, we saw benefits to all of the groups if we were to join. So,
15,01after all that jazz we have decided to drop all of the "COJ/EPI/SNC" junk and
15,01just say we are "The Alliance".

15,01         The Alliance was started when Black Magik and Mendelssohn were in
15,01chat on The Hierarchy. They were blabbing about something that made no sense
15,01whatsoever, as most chats go, when they came up with the idea of The Alliance.
15,01Since then many things have happened. The Alliance has gotten support from
15,01such people as Guile [ACiD], Faazy [Manifest], and Axe Battler [Shiver], and
15,01are on their way to international standings. We may not be the most talented
15,01artists but we are a dedicated group of artists and musicians and we are here
15,01to stay, we will not expunge into oblivion if we get flamed. So if you think
15,01we shouldn't be on the scene becasue we're not "talented" enough, oh well.. =)

15,01         The Senior members of The Alliance are Black Magik, Island of Reil,
15,01Mendelssohn, Nemesis, and Schizophreniac. We also have two advisors who give
15,01us a "viewers opinion" on what looks good and what doesn't, Black Aria and
15,01Digital Orgasm. There is no corporate ladder, all of the Senior members of the
15,01group do their work and have deadlines just like all of the other members of
15,01the group. The only thing that distinguishes the Seniors from the other
15,01artists/musicians is that they also decide what art/music is to be released in
15,01the pack, and they decide which applicants will be accepted. We, the Senior
15,01members, do all of this extra work but we don't think of ourselves as "above"
15,01the other members, we are also artists/musicians. We also take all of the
15,01members opinions into as much consideration as we take our own.

15,01         Now, you have heard in the beginning of this little newsletter about
15,01the Children of Judas, Epinicion, and Sonic. Well, in The Alliance, COJ and
15,01Sonic have permenantly merged, and most of the members of Epinicion have
15,01joined with us. COJ and Sonic are no longer, they, along with the majority of
15,01the Epinicion members, are forever "The Alliance". Epinicion is the premier
15,01music group in 619, and they are joining us as a seperate group because they
15,01have more plans for the future that The Alliance would interfere with, they
15,01plan on releasing demos and more musicdiscs.

15,01         Well, in the future you should expect a HELL of a lot more work and
15,01releases from us. We won't have scheduled releases due to the fact that
15,01our everyday lives get somewhat busy, and are more improtant than making art
15,01and music... =) Expect packs to be irregularly released with unscheduled
15,01side releases. Well, that's just about all we have to blab about for now, look
15,01for our releases and we will be glad to take any and all criticism on our
15,01work.

15,01                             Black Magik and Schizophreniac
