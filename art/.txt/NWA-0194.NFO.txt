15,01                                                        Logo by Hook

15,01        Hello everyone and I hope you enjoy the firs ever New Wave Artists
15,01        Art package. 

15,01        The origin of NWA was when I (The Narccissist) stepped down from my
15,01        presidents position in PAiN (for personal reasons) and it seemed
15,01        when I decided to start another group, all the PAiN artists were
15,01        loyal enough to me that they came with me. Following that, rival 604
15,01        art group (POiSON) and NWA merged to become what is thought of as 
15,01        the best art group to ever be based in 604.

15,01        The NWA art pack comes out on a regular basis on the 15th of each 
15,01        month, the reason we do not release on the 1st is because of other
15,01        groups that release theire packs on that day will most likely make 
15,01        ours forgotten in the crowd.

15,01        I'd like to say too all NWA members that it has been real great 
15,01        having the opportunity to work with all of you, and I'd like to give
15,01        out special greets to: Dreadnought, Juggernaut, all loyal Digital
15,01        Holocaust staff and users, and especially to Death Knight, the man
15,01        who made me think twice about just quitting the scene totally and 
15,01        who has been an inspiration in and outside the real world.

15,01                                                     The Narccissist
