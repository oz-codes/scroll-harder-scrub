00,01  151.00 Your Love              
00,01                             
00,01     My body is burnt, my soul is black;
00,01     Of all I have, your love I still lack.
00,01     Visage burned to memory, name to soul;
00,01     Alone I am torn, with you I am whole.

00,01     Without you I fade, slipping from the light;
00,01     To the mind's depth I fall, no end in sight.
00,01     You watch me plummet, your face a dark mask;
00,01     Love never dwindles, for help I won't ask.

00,01     My body strikes the ground, my sanity explodes;
00,01     I lay in a crimson heap, my eyes watch and forbode.
00,01     I silently cry for mercy, but my will falters not;
00,01     Yet I smile as I think of who I've eternally sought.

00,01     The blackness closes in I am lost and alone;
00,01     My eyelids close, the unknown is now known.
00,01     But when all is over and all is lost,
00,01     Over the boundaries of death a warm hand has crossed.

00,01     Mortality fills me with the red kiss of life;
00,01     Gone are the pain, the torture, the strife.
00,01     Now I am with you, and you are within;
00,01     Our love overcomes the black of my sins.

00,01     I smirk in rapture, a secret, curious smile;
00,01     In the grasp of your love I am charmed, beguiled.
00,01     Our hands and hearts clasped, my soul returns to me;
00,01     Together we shall create our own eternity.
00,01                    
15,01     Only poem written with a 'happy ending'.  Narrator is alone, missing
15,01     'her', a loved one.  She rejects him, and losing his mind, he finds
15,01     himself on the edge of death.  Just before dying, she comes to his
15,01     rescue ('over the boundaries .. has crossed').  Poem also hints at
15,01     how the love of two people can save a dark soul.  Last verse makes
15,01     remarks at how eternity is not found with god, it is found within
00,01 15    each other.

15,01     14Previously released in Hoard Magazine #1; RAGE Productions 08/94

00,01  152.00 I Shone 

00,01     The world tastes much better,
00,01     Now that I'm finally gone.
00,01     The waters are wetter,
00,01     And the words became a song.

00,01     She that I so loved,
00,01     Fled as I faded away.
00,01     Yet without blood, I bled,
00,01     When I died, I had no say.
00,01     
00,01     I'll never forget her,
00,01     And I'll wait for her sign.
00,01     My love tastes forever,
00,01     Her beauty words couldn't define.
