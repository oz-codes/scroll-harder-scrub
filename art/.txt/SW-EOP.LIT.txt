15,01- Eye of Peace -


15,01Darkness Falls
15,01As time is rent
15,01And the twilight of death descends.

15,01We stand in the rain
15,01Facing the lightning
15,01As the whirl of the storm begins.

15,01The blizzard awakens;
15,01The walls begin quaking
15,01And the weight of the storm transcends our belief.

15,01The hail cracks our windows
15,01As the streets flood with snow
15,01And our only thought is a prayer for relief.

15,01Then, in the moment when despair yokes us,
15,01The hush of silence somehow provokes us
15,01To see that we, in blessing, have found an oasis of peace.

15,01It is here In the Eye of the Blizzard
15,01That the chaos of life
15,01Finds its relief.
